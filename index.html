    Navigation help

    This module has audio narration. Ensure your headphones or speakers are operational. The following elements will help you navigate in this module:
    • Click Next to move to the next page.
    • Click Back to go back to the previous page.
    • Click Play/Pause to play or pause the visuals on the screen.
    • Click Replay/Refresh to play the screen again.
    • Click Audio On/Off to turn the audio on or off.
    • Click Transcript to view the audio transcript of the current screen.
    • Click Help to get navigation help on the module.
    • Click Resources to access the Philips GBP Toolkit with documents and intranet links for additional reading.
    • Click Exit to quit the module.
    • Drag Seek/Progress Bar to scroll through the content of the current screen.
