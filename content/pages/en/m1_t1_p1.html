Design Guidance Example

DESIGN PRINCIPLE

The main topic of the email and the purpose it serves is made clear at first sight.
DO THIS
State the topic and the purpose of the email transparently and easy to grasp for the reader in subject line, email teaser line & the emails intro section. Express the purpose of the email as an improved state, ability or advantage gained through the information given in the email.
AVOID THIS
Use subject line that do not resonate with the content of the email or neglects to nail down with a few words, what the email is about and why bayer is sending it. If you introduce the main topic, the topic is not connected to a positive outcome proposition to patients or the HCP themselves.
Header and introduction sums up key information
(WHAT / WHEN / WHERE)
Introduction highlights positive outcomes for the target audience using growth- and benefit oriented verbs
(e.g. learn how to improve)
